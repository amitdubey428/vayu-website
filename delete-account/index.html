Delete Your Account

This action is permanent and cannot be undone. All your trip data, preferences, and account information will be permanently removed.

How to delete your account:

Through the App

  1. Open the Vayu app on your device
  2. Go to your Profile settings
  3. Select "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm your decision
Open in Vayu App

Via Email Request

Send an email to our privacy team from your registered email address with the subject "Account Deletion Request".

Email privacy@vayutravels.com

Processing may take up to 7 business days.

What happens to your data?

  • All personal information is permanently deleted from our active databases
  • Trip data and shared content will be anonymized
  • Some data may remain in backups for up to 30 days before permanent deletion
